Privacy Policy - AIBrainIM

AIBrainIM is an AI collaboration platform mobile app developed by an individual developer.

Data Collection

  • Message Data: Your chat messages are used solely for AI dispatch purposes and are not uploaded to any third-party servers. All messages are processed locally.
  • Local Storage: Your messages, settings, and cache are stored on your device and are never uploaded to any server.
  • Crash Logs: Crash logs are used only for quality improvement purposes and are not shared with any third party.
  • Network Requests: The app sends messages to your self-hosted OpenClaw Gateway for AI dispatch. We do not send data to any other servers.

Data Security

  • Your data is processed by your own locally-controlled service
  • We do not collect, store, or sell any personal data
  • The app contains no third-party trackers or advertisements
