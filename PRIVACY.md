# Privacy Policy — AI Brain IM

**Last updated: 2026-05-07**

## Data We Collect

AI Brain IM ("we", "our", or "app") processes your messages and uploaded files through the OpenClaw AI platform to provide AI collaboration and task management services.

**Messages & Content:** Your text messages and attached files (images, videos, documents) are sent to OpenClaw Gateway for AI processing. This data is used solely to deliver the AI response and task dispatch services you request.

**Usage Data:** Agent runtime status, task states, and upload progress are tracked locally and synced with OpenClaw for real-time monitoring.

## How We Use It

- Deliver AI chat responses and dispatch instructions to agents
- Process and analyze uploaded files via AI
- Maintain task and memory state across sessions
- Generate real-time AI collaboration feeds

## Data Storage

- **On-Device:** Chat history is stored locally via AsyncStorage. Upload queue state is kept in memory.
- **OpenClaw Platform:** Messages and files are processed by OpenClaw agents as described at https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw

## Your Rights

You can clear all local data by:
- Using "Clear History" in the Chat screen
- Uninstalling the app (removes all local data)

For OpenClaw platform data, refer to the OpenClaw privacy policy.
